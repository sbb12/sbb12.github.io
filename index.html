Leads chrome extension

This is a simple Chrome extension which can be used to keep a track of potential leads, such as people on linked in or job postings on various websites. You can manually add leads to the list with the "Save input" button or click the "Save tab" button to save the current tab you are viewing. Double click "Delete All" to empty the list.
